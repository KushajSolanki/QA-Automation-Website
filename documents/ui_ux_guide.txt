UI/UX Guidelines

1. All form validation errors must be shown in RED.
2. The "Pay Now" button must be GREEN with white text.
3. Discount error messages must appear directly under the discount field.
4. Email field must validate email format.
5. Required fields: name, email, address.
6. Radio buttons must be visually grouped (Shipping, Payment).
7. After successful payment, success message must be displayed in bold.
